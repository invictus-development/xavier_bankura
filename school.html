THE SCHOOL

Welcome to St. Xavier's High School,Bankura. Affilliated to CBSE 10+2 Level.St. Xavier's High School is a new generation Co-Educational, English Medium School under the patronage of St. Francis Xavier. We aim to inspire every child to develop into a confident, well educated personable young adult who has enthusiasm for learning in a scientific environment where both the students and the teachers participate as friends in the process of learning. Modern teaching aids and a scientifically developed curriculum from every corner of education. St. Xavier's High School is a world in miniature from where we uphold traditional values and contemporary methodology with latest pedagogies. Our vision is to shape students mind with transformational experiences that will align their heart, mind and body. We strive to create a safe supportive and conducive environment where the abilities and individual differences of all the students are valued. We believe that Education being the strongest weapon should armor every young mind to face the challenges of the world and bring the best out of them. "Develop An Atitude Of Gratitude". Say Thank You To Everyone You Meet For Everything They Do For You"
